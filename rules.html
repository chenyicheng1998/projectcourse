Game Rules

  1. Starting Point: All players must start their journey from Singapore Changi Airport.
  2. Flight Distance Limit: Each flight leg cannot exceed 5,000 km.
  3. Weather Considerations:
    • Good Weather: You can land at the selected airport.
    • Bad Weather: Thunderstorms, heavy rain, or snow will prevent you from landing, and you must select an alternate destination.
  4. Crossing Longitude: During your journey, you must cross every longitudinal line.
  5. Eligible Airports: Players can only land at large, international airports with suitable facilities.
  6. Completing the Game: The round-the-world trip is completed when the player returns to Singapore Changi Airport.
  7. Scoring System: Players are ranked based on the total distance flown. The shorter the total distance, the better your rank.
  8. Replayability: Players can retry as many times as they want. The leaderboard will store your best attempts.
